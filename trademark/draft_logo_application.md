### 1. Description of the Mark (USPTO Form field)
> [!IMPORTANT]
> Paste the following exact text into the **"Description of the Mark"** field:

> *"The mark consists of a stylized, cute cartoon killer whale (orca). The whale is depicted in a swimming position, facing forward and down. *   **Wording to use:**

### 3. Official USPTO Design Search Codes
The USPTO examiner will use these codes to index your logo. You should list these codes in your application to speed up the indexing process:
*   **03.19.02:** Whales, killer whales (orcas)
*   **03.19.21:** Stylized sharks, whales, dolphins, and the like (covers cartoon/artistic renderings)
*   **02.11.02:** Human eyes (large circular cartoon eyes)
*   **02.11.04:** Human lips or mouths (smiling mouth)
*   **02.11.16:** Smiley faces (expression of the mascot)
