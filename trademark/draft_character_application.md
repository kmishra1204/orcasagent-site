## 📦 Section 2: Goods & Services Classifications

The USPTO is extremely strict about the descriptions (identifications) of goods and services. Using broad, vague terms will trigger an **Office Action** (a rejection/request for correction). 

The following clauses are custom-crafted, USPTO-compliant descriptions tailored specifically for an **Enterprise Agentic AI** product. You can copy-paste these wordings directly into your application.

### Class 9: Downloadable Software (Goods)
> [!NOTE]
> Select Class 9 if you distribute or intend to distribute local client apps, downloadable developer frameworks, local models, or desktop/mobile companion apps.
*   **Draft Identification (Copy & Paste):**
    > *"Downloadable computer software using artificial intelligence (AI) and autonomous agents for executing computer tasks, workflow automation, enterprise process orchestration, and data analysis; Downloadable computer software for designing, deploying, testing, managing, and monitoring autonomous AI software agents; Downloadable artificial intelligence (AI) software for business operations, business transformations, and workflow optimization."*

### Class 42: Software as a Service (Services)
> [!NOTE]
> Select Class 42 if you host the agentic platform in the cloud or offer a web portal where users log in and configure agents (SaaS/PaaS).
*   **Draft Identification (Copy & Paste):**
    > *"Software as a service (SaaS) services featuring software using artificial intelligence (AI) and autonomous agents for executing computer tasks, workflow automation, enterprise process orchestration, and data analysis; Software as a service (SaaS) services featuring software for designing, deploying, testing, managing, and monitoring autonomous AI software agents; Providing temporary use of online non-downloadable artificial intelligence (AI) software for business operations, business transformations, and workflow optimization."*

### Class 35 (Optional): Enterprise Business & Consulting Services
> [!NOTE]
> Select Class 35 if your business also provides professional human-led integration, consulting, or auditing services for other companies adopting agentic systems.
*   **Draft Identification (Copy & Paste):**
    > *"Business consulting services in the field of enterprise agentic transformations, namely, integrating autonomous artificial intelligence (AI) agents into business operations and workflows; Business process re-engineering and automation consulting using artificial intelligence and computer software."*
